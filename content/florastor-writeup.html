Why This One ‚Äî Post-Polyp Context

S. boulardii is a yeast, not a bacterium. This matters after a GI procedure. Bacterial probiotics (Lactobacillus, Bifido) can be disrupted by post-procedure antibiotics. S. boulardii is inherently antibiotic-resistant ‚Äî it survives and works even during antibiotic courses.
